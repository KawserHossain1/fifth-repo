Business Plan

Even if a company doesn't need additional funding, having a business plan helps it stay focused on its goals. Research from the University of Oregon shows that businesses with a plan are significantly more likely to secure funding than those without one. 1 Moreover, companies with a business plan grow 30% faster than those that don't plan. 2 According to a Harvard Business Review article, entrepreneurs who write formal plans are 16% more likely to achieve viability than those who don't. 3 A business plan should ideally be reviewed and updated periodically to reflect achieved goals or changes in direction. An established business moving in a new direction might even create an entirely new plan.
